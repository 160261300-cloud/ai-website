My Story

My name is Ebrahim and I am currently studying for an Access to Higher Education Diploma in Computing at Rotherham College. I chose AI as the topic for this website because it is the area of technology I find most exciting and relevant to the future.

What draws me to Artificial Intelligence is the way it combines logic, mathematics, and real-world problem solving. Whether it is a recommendation algorithm suggesting what to watch next, or a medical AI detecting cancer earlier than a doctor — the applications are endless and genuinely important.

This website is built entirely from scratch using HTML5 and CSS3. I deliberately avoided website builders like Wix or Squarespace because I wanted to understand how web pages actually work at a code level.
